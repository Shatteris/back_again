"Back Again" is a short text adventure. Due to personal limitations (and time) I'll aim for this to be a quick but hopefully enjoyable (and/or tolerable) adventure...
...maybe "trip" would be the better word.

Either way I hope it's passable. To begin just press on the button bellow. Enjoy and have a good day.

Just dark.

Been so for quite the while,
So much so that one finds themselfs doubting their eyes are even open...
EVEN after confirming this with touch.

One would think they'd be dead if it wasn't for their other senses;
The harsh and bumpy wall behind one's back that roughly fuses into an equally harsh floor...
Said floor that seems to be covered in a thick layer of a...pleasent thin powder. Such powder one's been twindling with their fingers to pass the time.

An... apreciated time burner...
But it's apreciation stops when said dust enters one's lungs...
Making itself known with each breath...
A constant reminder...
Of the passage of time.

A persistant annoyance.

Why are we still waiting?

get up.

So please...

...let's go.


The darkness doesn't leave just from standing... But at least we're able to wipe off all the dust clinging to our nethers.

And then another push...

Followed by other two on the opposite corner...

...and a last one in the middle!


No reaction, none at all...


Well...
We really don't have other option besides keep on pushing.

It's either that or wait for starvation to end us and that's gonna be a loooong wait...
Might as well exhaust ourself while we can.

So...

Ready?



With a rough breath we fall to our knees.

Crawling around our short "cell" patting it's surface. Maybe we'd find something to help breach the door.



After a while of blindly wiping dust everywhere like a demented mole we call it quits. Focusing our attention on the walls instead.
Maybe a loose pipe? A hole?


No... no such luck.


Positioning ourselfs to where we remenber the handle being we start to push.
And push.

Focusing on keeping a constant pressure on the door.
Switching from pushing with our back to pushing with our shoulders...
Our hands...
...a kick.


For a while this feels like a pathetic attempt at skinning ourselfs with it's rough surface until...

There!
It just give in a little!
Or is it gave out? No matter!
With renewed hope we keep pushing! Shifting from a constant push to quick, sudden ones whenever another give makes itself known

Slowly the door becomes looser until...
We suddenly dont feel it's presence anymore...

And collapse on the floor at the other side.


It feels embarassing...
Not because we fell
